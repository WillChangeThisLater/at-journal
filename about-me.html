About Me

Who I Am

Hi, I'm Paul! I'm a software engineer who recently quit my job to hike the entire Appalachian Trail. I've been planning this trip for years, and finally decided to take the plunge.

Why This Trail?

The AT has always fascinated me - 2,160 miles of mountain, forest, and river that stretches from Georgia to Maine. I wanted to experience the journey slowly, one mile at a time, and document the adventure along the way.
